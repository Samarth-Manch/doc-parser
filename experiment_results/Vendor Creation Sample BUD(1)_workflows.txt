APPROVER WORKFLOW (35 steps)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: For India, the workflow will consist of six sequential approval levels:
  Action Type: approve

Step 2: Pending with Requestor – Initial review and confirmation by the requestor.

Step 3: Pending with Vertical Head – Review and approval by the relevant business/vertical head.
  Action Type: approve

Step 4: Pending with Functional Head – Review and approval by the applicable functional head (e.g., Finance, Operations).
  Action Type: approve

Step 5: Pending with Accounts Team – Validation of accounting and finance-related information.
  Action Type: validate

Step 6: Pending with Auditor – Compliance and audit validation as required.
  Action Type: validate

Step 7: Pending with MDC Team – Final review by the MDC team. The MDC team can add ad hoc approvers as needed, up to a maximum of five parallel approvers. All ad hoc approvers must record their decisions, after which the request returns to the MDC approver, who then takes the final action in line with the defined common behaviour.
  Action Type: approve

Step 8: For Non-India, the workflow will consist of four sequential approval levels:
  Action Type: approve

Step 9: Pending with Requestor – Initial review and confirmation by the requestor. It is a contributor.

Step 10: Pending with Country Head – Review and approval by the respective country head.
  Action Type: approve

Step 11: Pending with Auditor – Compliance and audit validation as required.
  Action Type: validate

Step 12: Pending with MDC Team – Final review by the MDC team. The MDC team can add ad hoc approvers as needed, up to a maximum of five parallel approvers. All ad hoc approvers must record their decisions, after which the request returns to the MDC approver, who then takes the final action in line with the defined common behaviour.
  Action Type: approve
